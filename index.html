Even if I die let India win World Cup :- YUVI

Yuvi, the fighter:- Yuvraj played in the 2011 World Cup without realising he was fighting cancer, which was taking a massive toll on his body during the coveted tournament. Yuvraj was even seen coughing blood while batting in the middle of a match against the West Indies, but the renowned cricketer never considered leaving and continued to bat for India despite the pain. In the game, he scored a magnificent century and was named player of the match for his outstanding performance.

Winning the World Cup was very special because it meant so much to so many. One thing about our country that is constant is cricket. The smile it brought to people's faces was the thing I shall always remember.
